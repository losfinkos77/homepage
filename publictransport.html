Beginner tip: For Austria, start with ÖBB Scotty or BusBahnBim. Enter your start and destination, then compare travel time, transfers and walking distance.

Quick Austria Guide

  • For trains across Austria: use ÖBB Scotty.
  • For Styria, Graz, Weiz and regional buses: use BusBahnBim or Verbund Linie.
  • For Vienna: use Wiener Linien.
  • Always check platform, transfer time and live delay information before departure.
  • For tickets, compare ÖBB ticket shop, regional ticket options and Klimaticket if available.
