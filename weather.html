Photography weather tip: For landscape and wildlife photography, check rain radar, wind and cloud cover. For astrophotography, clouds and moonlight are the most important factors.

Quick Weather Guide

  • For official Austrian weather warnings: use GeoSphere Austria.
  • For rain before a photo trip: check rain radar.
  • For storms and lightning: use Lightning Maps.
  • For wind, clouds and big weather systems: use Windy.
  • For hiking and mountain weather: use Bergfex.
  • For Milky Way and night photography: check Clear Outside and moonlight.
